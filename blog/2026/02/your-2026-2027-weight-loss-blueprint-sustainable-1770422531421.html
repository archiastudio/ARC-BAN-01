5 Sustainable Habits for 2026/2027 Success

1. Prioritize Protein & Fiber

Incorporate these into every meal:

  • Lentils, chickpeas, or grilled chicken
  • Leafy greens, berries, or whole grains

2. Hydrate Smartly

  • Start your day with warm lemon water
  • Swap sugary drinks for herbal teas

3. Master Portion Control

Use the "plate method":

  • 50% vegetables
  • 25% lean protein
  • 25% whole grains

4. Plan Weekly Meals

Avoid impulsive eating with these steps:

  • Dedicate Sundays to meal prep
  • Batch-cook staples like quinoa or roasted veggies

5. Mindful Eating Practices

  • Chew slowly and savor each bite
  • Limit distractions during meals
