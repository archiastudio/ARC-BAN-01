Understanding Genetic Nutrition Testing

Genetic nutrition testing analyzes specific variations in your DNA that influence how your body processes, utilizes, and responds to nutrients. These tests identify genetic markers related to metabolic pathways, food sensitivities, vitamin requirements, and predispositions to certain health conditions that may be influenced by diet.

  • SNP Analysis - Examines single nucleotide polymorphisms that affect nutrient metabolism
  • Metabolic Pathway Evaluation - Assesses how efficiently your body processes carbohydrates, fats, and proteins
  • Vitamin and Mineral Utilization - Identifies genetic variations that impact nutrient absorption and utilization
  • Food Sensitivity Markers - Detects genetic predispositions to adverse reactions to certain foods
  • Disease Risk Assessment - Evaluates genetic links between diet and chronic disease risk
