2. Create a Sustainable Meal Blueprint

Your meal plan will focus on whole foods, balanced macros, and seasonal ingredients. Sample framework:

3. Incorporate Smart Snack Strategies

Smart snacks keep hunger in check without sabotaging progress:

  • Protein shake or cottage cheese with pineapple.
  • Whole grain toast with avocado.

4. Hydration and Mindful Eating Techniques

  • Aim for 2–2.5 liters of water daily.
  • Practice portion control with smaller plates.

  • 3–4 strength sessions per week focusing on major muscle groups.
  • 30 minutes of moderate cardio 5 times a week.

  • Monthly weight and body‑fat milestones.
