        Get Answers to Your Questions

        Is a dietitian and nutritionist the same thing?
        No, they are not the same. A dietitian is a registered healthcare professional with specific education and training, while a nutritionist may or may not have formal qualifications.
        Do I need a doctor's referral to work with a YashPal
        Usually, you don't need a doctor's referral to see a dietitian. However, if you're using health insurance, your provider might require one for coverage.
        How long does a consultation with a archiastudio dietitian last?
        A typical initial consultation with a dietitian can last anywhere from 45 minutes to an hour. Follow-up appointments may be shorter, depending on your needs and progress.
        How often should I schedule follow-up consultations?
        The frequency of follow-up consultations depends on your individual needs and goals. However, a common approach is to schedule appointments every 2-4 weeks initially. As you progress and become more comfortable with your plan, the frequency can be reduced to once a month or even less. Your dietitian will work with you to determine the best schedule to support your success.
